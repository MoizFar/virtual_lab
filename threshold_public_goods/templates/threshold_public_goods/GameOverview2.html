You and your group members receive tokens from any of the two group accounts if and only if the number of tokens allocated to each group account is greater than or equal to its pre-specified number that is called “threshold”.

Group Account A:


The threshold for Group Account A is at least {{Constants.threshold_high}} tokens.

If the total amount contributed by you and your group members to Group Account A is at least {{Constants.threshold_high}} tokens to this group account, you and your group members will each earn {{Constants.value_high}} tokens from this account. Otherwise, nobody earns any tokens from this account.

Group Account B:


The threshold for Group Account A is at least {{Constants.threshold_low}} tokens.

If the total amount contributed by you and your group members to Group Account B is at least {{Constants.threshold_low}} tokens to this group account, you and your group members will each earn {{Constants.value_low}} tokens from this account. Otherwise, nobody earns any tokens from this account.
